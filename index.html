A Guiding Story

The Russian Cosmonaut

The Cosmonaut goes up in a big space ship, and in this space ship is a portal window. He looks out this portal window and for the first time ever, he can see the curvature of the Earth. A beautiful moment. All of a sudden, a ticking sound starts protruding from the dashboard. He can't find the noise, he doesn't know where it's coming from, he keeps going. After a few hours of this, it begins to feel like torture. But what is he going to do? He's in space! The Cosmonaut decides that the only way to save his sanity, is to fall in love with the sound.

Background

About Me

I'm Dylan Bozada — a data enthusiast who loves spending time outside. When I'm not working with data, I'm usually on a trail or in a canoe somewhere. I've always believed the best way to recharge is to get off the screen entirely.

I work primarily in the data engineering space, building and maintaining pipelines that turn raw data into something useful. My toolkit includes Python, SQL, PySpark, and Airflow for orchestration, with hands-on experience in Databricks and Azure for cloud-scale processing. I'm also comfortable working with Docker to keep environments clean and deployments consistent. Recently I've been exploring AI tooling as well, with some experience in LangGraph and Arize Ax.
