A Guiding Story

The Russian Cosmonaut

The Cosmonaut goes up in a big space ship, and in this space ship is a portal window. He looks out this portal window and for the first time ever, he can see the curvature of the Earth. A beautiful moment. All of a sudden, a ticking sound starts protruding from the dashboard. He can't find the noise, he doesn't know where it's coming from, he keeps going. After a few hours of this, it begins to feel like torture. But what is he going to do? He's in space! The Cosmonaut decides that the only way to save his sanity, is to fall in love with the sound.

Background

About Me

I'm Dylan Bozada — a data enthusiast who spends his free time on Missouri's rivers, chasing smallmouth bass and ticking off miles on the water.

I use Python and data analysis to make sense of the world around me, whether that's tracking a summer paddling challenge or exploring a new dataset. This site is where I share those projects.

When I'm not writing code, you'll find me in a canoe.

100+ Miles paddled on Missouri rivers in Summer 2022
7 Rivers explored: Big, Black, Current, Missouri, Niangua, St. Francis, Huzzah
5.5 lb Smallmouth — lunker board record (Big River, July 2022)
